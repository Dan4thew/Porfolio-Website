About Me

Daniel is a passionate UI/UX Designer with a knack for creating intuitive user experiences. He has a Bachelor's degree in Computer science and over a year of professional experience as a systems associate and designing engaging user interfaces.

Daniel is skilled in Adobe Xd, Figma, HTML5, and CSS, and is always eager to learn new technologies. He has a love for problem-solving and creating simple, yet effective, solutions. With his creative eye, Daniel is always looking for ways to refine and enhance the user experience.

He is also experienced in working collaboratively in a team setting and has a proven track record in coming up with innovative solutions. His goal is to continue to grow as a designer and create engaging and impactful designs.

In his spare time, Daniel enjoys exploring new technologies and staying up to date on the latest trends. He also enjoys traveling, listening to music, and watching football.
